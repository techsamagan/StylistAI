Motivation

The Problem

🌧️

Weather Blindness

People pick outfits without checking the forecast — underdressed in rain, overdressed in heat.

📅

Context Mismatch

Casual clothes to a formal meeting, or overdressed for a relaxed brunch with friends.

🤔

"What Do I Wear?"

Decision fatigue every morning — even with a full wardrobe, choosing takes 10–20 minutes daily.

StylistAI solves all three — by combining your closet, the weather, and your schedule into one smart recommendation.

Overview

What is StylistAI?

  • Digitise your wardrobe — upload and categorise every clothing item
  • Set your city once in your Profile — powers everything automatically
  • Daily outfit auto-generated using live weather + calendar formality
  • Outfit Generator with context, vibe slider, and weather awareness
  • AI Wardrobe Gap Analysis — spots what's missing and explains why
  • Smart Shopping Suggestions based on your wardrobe + local weather
  • Travel Packer — per-day outfit plans + deduplicated packing list
  • Advanced Virtual Try-On powered by OpenAI DALL·E 3
📍

One Setting, Everything Works

You type your city once in your profile. The entire app — weather, outfits, shopping tips — personalises itself around it instantly.

🤖

AI at Every Step

From outfit picking to gap analysis to try-on images — AI is embedded throughout, not bolted on.

Feature 3

Context

Feature 5

Travel Packer

  • Enter destination city + number of days
  • Fetches live weather for that destination via Open-Meteo
  • Adds outerwear automatically for cold destinations (<18°C)
  • Generates a unique outfit for each day from your closet
  • Deduplicates all items into one master packing list
  • Shows weather summary alongside the plan
🗺️

Smart Deduplication

Same jacket for day 1 and 3? Appears in the packing list once. Pack light, pack smart.

# POST /travel/pack { "destination": "Paris", "days": 4 } # Response { "weather_summary": "9.4°C in Paris", "packing_list": [ /* unique items only */ ], "daily_outfits": [ { "day": 1, "items": [ ... ] }, { "day": 2, "items": [ ... ] }, { "day": 3, "items": [ ... ] }, { "day": 4, "items": [ ... ] } ] }
Feature 6

AI — Try-On & Smart Shopping

🖼️

Advanced Virtual Try-On

Select any generated outfit → hit Try On → OpenAI DALL·E 3 generates a photorealistic full-body portrait wearing those exact clothes in your chosen context — Office, Date Night, Travel and more.

POST /outfits/try-on → outfit items + context → prompt → DALL·E 3 (1024×1024) → photorealistic portrait returned
🛍️

Smart Shopping Suggestions

The app analyses your wardrobe categories and formality balance, then recommends exactly what to buy next — and tells you how many new outfit combinations that one purchase will create.

📍

Location-Powered Suggestions

Shopping tips factor in your city's climate. If you live somewhere cold, the app will push you toward outerwear. Warm city? It knows you don't need a heavy coat.

☁️

Weather-Aware Outfit Logic

Rain codes, temperature thresholds (hot >25°C, cold <15°C), and layering — all handled automatically from your saved city. No manual input ever.

💡

Gap Analysis Intelligence

Ratio-based AI: "You have a 5× top-to-bottom ratio — one pair of pants instantly creates 5 new outfit combinations." Specific. Actionable. Saves you money.
